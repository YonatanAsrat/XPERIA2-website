About Xperia

Born from a genuine passion for redefining how people experience life, Xperia was created to fill a void in the Ethiopian experience landscape. We envision gatherings as transformative journeys that touch the soul and leave an indelible memory.

Our mission is to deliver more than just gatherings by redefining experiences through predictive personalization, immersive multisensory encounters, and holistic theme integration.

Enebla Catering

Overview

Enebla is a specialized one-stop experiential service dedicated exclusively to children's celebrations. We integrate themed culinary arts with immersive entertainment and global sourcing to create seamless experiences for modern parents.

Our Mission

To transform children's events by executing hyper-personalized, themed catering that marries balanced meals with logistically seamless entertainment.

Our Vision

To become the regional benchmark for immersive children's experiences, where every child feels like the hero of their own story.

MenYeqrab Foods

Overview

MenYeqrab is a hybrid meal kit and digital food platform that merges ready-made convenience with personalized meal planning. We provide everything from raw ingredients and pre-prepped kits to chef-cooked meals and student lunchboxes.

Our Mission

To empower families to enjoy hassle-free, nutritious meals tailored to their lifestyle through innovative meal plans and a supportive culinary community.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform that bridges the gap between convenience and home-cooked meals, fostering healthier eating habits and stronger cooking skills.
